THE STORY OF OUR PROJECT

Our project's focus was to explore the applications of artificial intelligence, in new and experimental methods. We took on the task of creating board games, on our topic of climate change. To this goal, we used many different AI tools, ranging from image generation AIs, to language models like Chat GPT and Google Bard.

GETTING TO KNOW EACH OTHER

Before starting to create the board games, we first played some energizers and participated in workshops to get to know each other, creating a safe and comfortable environment to learn and experiment together as a team.

The Board Games & Scouts Event

On the last day of our project, we invited the young and enthusiatic members of the Scouts Center in Sanem to be the first to test our games, as well as other surprises. At first, the language barrier seemed like a challenge, but we overcame it by showing our new scouts friends national dances from our home countries. We also prepared food and sweets from home, and invited the scouts to taste the best tiramisu from Italy, the sweetest fruits pierogi from Poland, Romanian polenta with sour cream and traditional cheese, and the best sweets Estonia. Our Luxembourigh friends also prepared the traditional Gromperekichelcher, a type of fried and flattened potato.
