About the Show

ART Meets AI is a unique theatrical experiment born from an Erasmus youth exchange held in Krosno, Poland. Our group, consisting of 28 participants from Romania, Poland, Slovakia, and the Czech Republic, embarked on an extraordinary journey to create a show directed and enhanced by AI. This pioneering project blends live on-stage action with cutting-edge AI techniques, including voice modulation, deepfaking, and the generation of captivating images and music.
We were proud to present our AI show in front of 600 students and teachers from the schools in Krosno.
The project is co-founded by the European Union.

Our Mission

Our mission is to push the boundaries of traditional theatre by integrating advanced AI technologies into live performances. We aim to demonstrate how AI can augment human creativity, leading to new forms of artistic expression and storytelling.

Enhanced with AI

At ART Meets AI, we harness the power of artificial intelligence to revolutionize creative arts. By integrating AI into our theatrical performances, we explore innovative techniques like real-time voice modulation, deepfake technology, and AI-generated imagery and music. This synergy between human creativity and machine intelligence pushes the boundaries of artistic expression, creating a unique, immersive experience. Join us as we redefine storytelling with AI-enhanced artistry.

Snow White's AI Journey

The untimely death of Krosno's King, caused by a high-tech self-driving car from Elon Musk, ignites a thrilling power struggle. The Evil Queen seizes this chance to push her twisted democracy, while Snow White, the grieving princess, fights back with the help of AI-enhanced allies. As both sides engage in a high-stakes battle of misinformation and manipulation, the story dives deep into the ethical complexities of technology and power...
